About

Ian Wallis.

Founder, Peebles

Ian Wallis is the founder of Peebles, an independent strategy and onchain delivery consultancy on tokenisation, security and go-to-market. He spent the previous chapter of his career at Consensys, where he was Head of Business Development, leading MetaMask business development, building the MetaMask security programme, taking the Linea L2 from launch to over $1.3B in TVL, launching the $Linea token and building the Linea Consortium treasury.

Before crypto he held enterprise sales leadership roles at Oracle and Pega, and began his career as a management consultant at Accenture. He writes and speaks on the decentralised models the next economy will run on.
